Meet wavedox

Wavedox (spelled in lower case, "wavedox") is a free resource for DC Universe Online (DCUO) players who are looking to make progress with their characters, leagues and groups, based on open data from Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) Census.

It was known as DCUO Social Graph, and hosted under http://dcuo.herokuapp.com. The new name is inspired by the awesome raid Paradox Wave, and the logo design is a courtesy of BumblingB.

Suggested Feats

Suggested Feats

Suggested Feats is the personal dashboard of every DCUO player who's working on feats, from level one exploration feats to the newest DLC. It predicts and suggests which feats you should do next. Suggestions are calculated based on overall completeness.

The more a feat has been completed by others, the more Potential (%) you have to complete that feat. Suggested Feats are sorted by Potential (%), showing you the ones you're more likely to complete first.

Leaderboards

Leaderboards are available for current/max stats only, meaning it includes additional amounts to base stats, e.g. trinkets, colas, buffs. A lot of people have asked to rank only on base stats, but unfortunately Census doesn't provide base stats yet.

I'll soon put together everyone's feedback on base stats and make sure it's addressed to the Census team. For now enjoy the dynamic Leaderboards, or wait until base stats are available.
