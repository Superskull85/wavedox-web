Meet wavedox

Wavedox (spelled in lower case, "wavedox") is a free resource for DC Universe Online (DCUO) players who are looking to make progress with their characters, leagues and groups, based on open data from Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) Census.

It was known as DCUO Social Graph, and hosted under http://dcuo.herokuapp.com. The new name is inspired by the awesome raid Paradox Wave.
